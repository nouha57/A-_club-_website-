Hosted by IEEE Geoscience and Remote Sensing Society (IEEE GRSS), the Mediterranean and Middle-East Geoscience and Remote Sensing Symposium 2020 (M2GARSS 2020) was held from March 9th to March 11th, 2020 in Tunis, Tunisia. The IEEE GRSS and the M2GARSS 2020 Steering Committee, were pleased to invite their participants to M2GARSS 2020 that was be the 1st Mediterranean and Middle-East (M2) regional symposium and contributed to gather M2 and world-class scientists, engineers and educators engaged in the fields of geoscience, remote sensing and related topics.

M2GARSS provided an ideal forum for obtaining up-to-date information about the latest developments, exchanging ideas, identifying future trends in your research area and making contacts with the international remote sensing community. On the second day there were the Parallel Oral Sessions and the parallel Poster Sessions where participants nearly from all over the world came and presented their work and posters. Each participant projected his work and tried to define a specific problem related to his study field and the theme of GRSS and then presented the required results and solutions according to him, in a big meeting room and in front of judges, they were given limited time slots and their presentations were eventually evaluated by the judges. we can't deny it was so challenging especially during the covid-19 epidemic, but the best thing that in the poster presentations there where remote ones using the ZOOM application, each presenter also had a time slot of 3 minutes, 3 slides to present his main ideas, results in that short time and after all the remote poster presentations there where a 10 minutes audience interventions and questionnaire.
